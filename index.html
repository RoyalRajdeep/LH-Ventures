About Us

We Invest in Early Stage Ventures and Build Companies Across Multiple Industries

We invest early stage ventures that provide simple, effective and innovative solution to real world problems and have realistic execution plans for monetizing their solution. We are flexible in our investment approach and prefer to diversify investments across different industries, as long as the opportunity is right and we can add value. Our past investments span from information technology, biotechnology, medical device, consumer products, to engineering services and business services. We are actively looking for companies that have gained actual traction and evident momentum in the marketplace and are ready to take off.

In addition to investing in ventures, we apply our knowledge and experience to constantly build and operate in-house companies until they scale into self-sustainable and profitable businesses.

Lead UX Designer

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with product management and engineering to define and implement innovative solutions for the product direction, visuals and experience
  • Execute all visual design stages from concept to final hand-off to engineering
  • Create wireframes, storyboards, user flows, process flows and site maps to effectively communicate interaction and design ideas
  • Present and defend designs and key milestone deliverables to peers and executive level stakeholders
  • Conduct user research and evaluate user feedback

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in Photoshop, Illustrator, OmniGraffle, or other visual design and wireframing tools
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for rapid prototyping
  • Up-to-date with the latest UI trends, techniques, and technologies
  • Experience working in an Agile/Scrum development process
  • BS/MS in Human-Computer Interaction, Interaction Design, or related

Front-End Developer
