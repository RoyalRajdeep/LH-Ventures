What We Do LH Ventures

We Invest in Early Stage Companies and Found Ventures Accross Multiple Industries

We invest in early stage companies that provide simple, effective and innovative solution to real world problems and have realistic execution plans for monetizing their solution. We are flexible in our investment approach and prefer to diversify investments across different industries, as long as the opportunity is right and we can add value. Our past investments span from information technology, biotechnology, medical device, consumer products, to engineering services and business services. In the due diligence process, we regard the actual tractions gained in the marketplace more important than other key elements such as management team, technology innovation, competitive advantage, and financials.

  • Invests in early stage companies
  • Focused on market and customer validation
  • Sales traction is the ideal form of market validation

In-house Ventures

In addition to investing and building local startups, we apply our knowledge and experience to constantly found and operate a number of in-house ventures in multiple verticals, until they scale into self-sustainable and profitable companies.horizons. We make investments in companies based upon the attractiveness of the opportunity and our ability to contribute value..
